About Jodo Kitchen

Located in the vibrant heart of Accra's Spintex area, Jodo's Kitchen is a beloved local eatery renowned for its delectable blend of African and continental cuisines. Situated near the Golden Simcha Hotel on Coastal Down, we offer a warm and inviting atmosphere where guests can enjoy a diverse menu crafted to satisfy every palate. Whether you're in the mood for a hearty local dish or a continental favorite, our kitchen is dedicated to delivering flavorful meals made with the finest ingredients. We pride ourselves on providing exceptional service and a dining experience that feels like home. Operating from Tuesday to Sunday, 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM, we cater to both dine-in and take-out preferences.
